# Evolution of Speech Synthesis

- **1980s-1990s:** Formant & Concatenative Synthesis (Stiff, robotic, required massive databases).
- **2016-2018 (WaveNet/Tacotron 2):** Introduction of Deep Learning and Attention Models (High quality, but slow inference).
- **2023 (OpenVoice):** Introduced tone color conversion and embeddings.
- **Present (Parrot-AI / Qwen3-TTS):** Unified dual-track LALM architectures capable of zero-shot cloning and real-time generation.
