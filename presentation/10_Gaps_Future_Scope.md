# Limitations & Future Scope

**Current Gaps:** 
- Heavy reliance on local GPU VRAM.
- Potential ethical risks regarding deepfake voice spoofing.

**Future Scope:**
- **Model Compression:** Optimizing weights for edge deployment (mobile devices).
- **Conversational AI:** Integrating with LLMs for real-time, low-latency AI voice agents.
- **Security:** Implementing digital watermarking to identify AI-generated audio.
