# The Limitations of Traditional TTS

- **Robotic Delivery:** Legacy systems lack emotional depth and contextual prosody.
- **Data Heavy:** Traditional voice cloning requires hours of studio recording and expensive model retraining.
- **High Latency:** Processing long scripts in neural models often leads to timeouts or unresponsive user interfaces.
